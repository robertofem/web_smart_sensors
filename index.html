Electronic Technology Department (DTE)

Summary of the project

DTE smart sensors project is a research project lead by Electronic Technology Department (DTE) of the University of Vigo and focused on the development of smart sensors using reconfigurable hardware platforms like FPGAs and FPSOCs. Nowadays.... It is founded by the Spanish government under the grant XXXXXXXXXXX and supported by several partners and contributors.

About the DTE

The Electronic Technology Department of the University of Vigo was born in 1985. Since the beginning it has combined the educative tasks with research in areas like microelectronics, sensors, electronic products and lifecycle, power electronics, etc.
